Our Site Purpose

Our purpose is to provide weather, water, and climate data, forecasts and warnings for the protection of life and property and enhancement of the national economy.

It means communities across the country are ready, responsive and resilient to weather, water and climate threats. Community decision makers have access to information that is understandable and prepares them to take action to safeguard lives and livelihoods ahead of the onset of dangerous conditions.
