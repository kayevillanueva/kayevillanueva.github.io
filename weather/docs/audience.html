Our Target Audience

It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has the mobile phone weather app. The people who mainly use this service are people who are booking holidays to check the weather in a different country. It is also used by anyone who wants to go on a fun day out.

Personas

Persona 1 – George: George is a 50 year old father of a family of five. They are planning a trip to go to the Bahamas. He wanted to check the weather on the following couple weeks to see if there's any hurricane coming up. That way, they could decide what the perfect day is to take days off from work. Persona 2 – Georgina: Georgina is a 29 year old auditor for one of the Big 4 Accounting Firms.
