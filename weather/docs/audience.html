Our Target Audience

It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has the mobile phone weather app. The people who mainly use this service are people who are booking holidays to check the weather in a different country. It is also used by anyone who wants to go on a fun day out.

Personas

Persona 1 – George: George is a 50 year old father of a family of five. They are planning a trip to go to the Bahamas. He wanted to check the weather on the following couple weeks to see if there's any hurricane coming up. That way, they could decide what the perfect day is to take days off from work.

Persona 2 – Georgina: Georgina is a 29 year old auditor for one of the Big 4 Accounting Firms. So, she travels a lot for work. She checks her mobile weather app every time she flies out to audit some client companies out of state.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Kayla needs to know if it is going to be snowing overnight. He needs to know what outfit he needs to prepare for tomorrow's work.

Scenario 2: Lee needs to know when he should change into snow tires soon. He checks the weather every so often.

Scenario 3: Barry travels a lot for work. He needs to plan wisely his flight details so he could avoid any delayed flights now that it is hurricane season.

Scenario 4: Danny owns five pigs. He wants to know when the temperature is going to go down so he could buy and get some hay for his pigs.

Scenario 5: May wants to go skiing with her friends. She wants to know when the best time to do it.
